Workpackages

WP1: Conceptual Framework (Kyoto U), Months 1-6

To specify details of the proposed framework resilience frameworks such as the 4R model (robustness,redundancy, resourcefulness and rapidity) will be reviewed. Further, other studies regarding behavioural changes, anxiety during Covid 19 (meta study) will be reviewed as well as economic indicators.
